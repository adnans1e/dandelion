Hello ! Welcome to this web page about project Dandelion. It is here to centralize the information and help you find the latest documents. If you are interested in helping, please get in touch.

What is project Dandelion

Project Dandelion is our Whole-Community Approach to Well-Being & Learning for Refugee Children. It's a design research project initiated in 2016, currently being prototyped and created by the IRC's education team and R&D team. It is led by the International Rescue Committee (IRC). Learn more about our work here .
